About The Company

Engineering Vertical Movement For Pakistan's Skyline

AL HADI ELEVATORS is a Peshawar-based elevator engineering company delivering precision-built vertical transportation systems for homes, hospitals, plazas and commercial towers across Pakistan. Led by Hadiullah Marwat, our team of licensed engineers manages every stage in-house — from site survey and design through installation, testing and lifetime maintenance.

We don't just install elevators — we engineer trust, one floor at a time.

Our Mission

To engineer safe, reliable and elegant vertical transportation systems that move Pakistan's buildings — and the people inside them — forward.

Our Vision

To be Pakistan's most trusted name in elevator engineering — known for precision installation and rapid emergency response.
